ON A TRAM


The No. 1 South Melbourne tram goes right past our door. Get
off at Stop 18. Any tram down St Kilda Road—jump off at
Grant Street, Stop 17 and take a 3-minute walk.




ON A TRAIN


Any train to Flinders Street, then a 12-minute stroll through
Melbourne’s sparkling Arts Precinct.

PARKING OPTIONS


Limited on-street parking is available on Grant Street, Sturt
Street and Dodds Streets.
Two disabled parking spaces are just outside The Coopers Malthouse entrance on Sturt Street.



The closest secure carparks are: Arts Centre Melbourne Car Park (enter from Sturt Street) The Australian Ballet Centre Car Park (2 Kavanagh Street)
